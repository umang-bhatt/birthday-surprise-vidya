A Letter For You

A Letter for the Girl Who Feels Deeply

Dear You,

14th June... shayad duniya ke liye bas ek aur date ho, lekin mere liye yeh woh din hai jab meri zindagi ko uski sabse khoobsurat wajah mili. Kehte hai jodi upar vaala banata hai, mere liye bhagwan ne aapko iss dharti pe aajke din bheja ❤️

Pata hai, aapki sabse khaas baat kya hai? Aap internal aur external dono same ho: simple and sweet. Aapka pyaar, aapki care aur aap har rishte ko dil se nibhate ho.... yeh sab aapko bahut alag aur bahut special banata hai.

Thank you for being not just the love of my life, but also my best friend. Woh insaan jiske saath main bina kisi darr ke apni har baat share kar sakta hoon. Jiske saath silence bhi comfortable lagta hai aur conversations kabhi khatam nahi hoti. Dhire dhire aap mujhe jaan-ne lage ho, aur mai bhi yehi stage pe hu.

Aapke saath maine seekha hai ki pyaar sirf grand gestures mein nahi hota; woh un choti choti baaton mein hota hai— "khana khaya?", "ghar pahunch gaye?", "apna dhyaan rakhna." Aur aap har roz mujhe yeh ehsaas dilati hain ki sachcha pyaar kaisa hota hai.

Aap meri zindagi ka sukoon hain, meri sabse badi strength hain, aur meri favourite "good morning" aur "good night" hain. Mere har plan mein, har dua mein, aur har future dream mein aap hain.

Aaj aapka special day hai, aur meri dua hai ki aapki life hamesha khushiyon, pyaar, achhi health, aur endless blessings se bhari rahe. Aapki woh pyari si smile kabhi kam na ho, aur aap hamesha wahi amazing insaan bani rahen jisse maine pyaar kiya hai.

Thank you for being patient with me, understanding me, supporting me, guiding me and choosing me every single day.

Happy Birthday, maari jaanudi ❤️
Happy Birthday, maari golgappi ❤️
Happy Birthday, maari chapli ❤️
Happy Birthday, maari waydi ❤️

Tere nakhre, teri khamoshiyan, sab kuch hi lagta hai pyaara
Khudko dhoodhna hai tere har ek ishare mai saara ka saara
Tere pyaarka nasha kuch aisa chaaya
Dil mera mai tujhme hi hai samaya
Kon kehta hai pyaar ek baar hota hai
Mai jab bhi dekhu tumhe, mujhe hota ek aur baar hai.
Na bandhan hai, na phere hai
Bas ek ehsaas hai jisme hum tere hai.

Me to god:
Koi kisi mai reh gaya
Koi kisi mai beh gaya
Koi duaao mai mangta reh gaya
Aur koi kisiko bin maange hi mil gaya

No matter where life takes us, one thing will always remain the same — I will always be grateful that our paths crossed, we blossomed into love, and that I get to call you mine.

And thank you for being just exactly the way you are.

– From someone who found his home in you. ❤️
- Mr. Atropine
